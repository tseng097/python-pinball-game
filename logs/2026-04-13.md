# 2026-04-13

## Observed issues
- Launcher could be charged/released anywhere on the playfield, letting players add vertical impulse mid-game (exploit, unrealistic behavior).
- No explicit lane gating for the launcher meant ball physics could be altered outside the plunger lane.
- Lacked a headless check ensuring launcher impulse only applies in the launch lane.

## Changes
- Added launcher lane bounds and helper functions to gate impulses to the lane.
- Launcher charge now only accumulates while the ball is in the lane; otherwise it resets.
