## Observed issues
- Physics step allowed a relatively large max dt (1/30) with only 3 substeps, which increases tunneling risk at high ball speeds or during frame spikes.
- Repeated bumper impacts could push the ball toward the velocity cap; there was no automated guard verifying the cap holds during sustained bumper collisions.
- Headless coverage didn’t explicitly assert bumper chains keep speed within MAX_BALL_SPEED.

## Changes
- Reduced physics max dt and increased substeps to stabilize fast collisions.
- Added a headless test to ensure repeated bumper bounces keep the ball speed capped.
